Support Programs

Discover the programs below specifically designed to assist seniors.

State Pension: This is a regular payment from the government for people who have reached their state age pension.

Pension Credit: This provides extra money to those on low income.

Housing Benefit: This helps seniors on low income with the cost of their rent, by reducing their cost.

Winter Fuel Payment: This is a annual tax payment to help seniors with the cost of heating during winter.

Free Bus Pass: This helps seniors get around by having free travel on local buses, helping them complete their daily tasks without worrying about travel costs.

PIP (Personal Independence Payment): This is a benefit for people, including seniors with long-term health conditions or disabilities.
